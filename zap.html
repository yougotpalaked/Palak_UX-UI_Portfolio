Tap. Done. Repeat.

A mobile app designed for fast, gesture-driven everyday actions — built to make the most common tasks feel effortless.

ZAP was built around speed and clarity: a single-tap entry into core flows, a quiet interface that gets out of the way, and a system that prioritises what users do most. The result is an app that feels less like a tool and more like a reflex.
