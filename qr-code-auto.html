Designing for the last mile.

A UX research study on effective QR code placement in auto-rickshaws, exploring how commuters interact with payment and information touchpoints in transit.

Auto-rickshaws are one of India's most used transit modes, yet their digital touchpoints are often poorly placed and rarely tested. This research investigated where QR codes actually get noticed, scanned, and ignored - producing placement guidelines grounded in real commuter behaviour.
