Designing under observation.

A UX research project evaluating AI-driven hiring tools, assessing how design thinking holds up under AI-moderated assessment conditions.

By participating in and documenting the process, the research surfaces what AI evaluators prioritise, where they fall short, and how designers can communicate their thinking more effectively in high-stakes, time-pressured environments.
