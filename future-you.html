Clarity for what comes next.

Beta testing the Future U app to improve user experience and engagement for students exploring career paths and personal development.

The project required understanding how students think about their own futures - an inherently ambiguous subject. The design challenge was creating an experience that felt both encouraging and grounded, guiding users through self-reflection without overwhelming or patronising them.
