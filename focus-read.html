Read more. Drift less.

A browser extension designed to enhance reading focus and comprehension by reducing visual noise and guiding attention through long-form content.

FocusRead was built around a single insight: the hardest part of reading online isn't finding content, it's staying with it. The extension strips away distractions at a browser level, giving readers a calmer, more intentional experience without changing the content itself.
